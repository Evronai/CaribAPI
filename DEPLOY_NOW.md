## **📞 Need Help?**

**Quick troubleshooting:**
1. **Check logs:** Platform dashboard → Deployments → Logs
2. **Test locally first:** `python test_minimal.py`
3. **Verify database:** Platform provides `DATABASE_URL` automatically

**Common issues:**
- **"Module not found"** - Wait for build to finish (2-3 min)
- **"Database error"** - Wait 30-60 seconds after deployment
- **"Port error"** - Platform handles ports automatically
- **"Service sleeping"** (Render free tier) - First request may be slow

# CaribAPI Business Plan

## 🎯 Executive Summary

**CaribAPI** is a fully automated API platform providing Caribbean business data to developers, businesses, and researchers. We solve the problem of fragmented, inaccessible Caribbean business information by offering a unified, real-time API.

### Key Metrics:
- **MVP Development Time:** 1 hour (completed)
- **Time to First Revenue:** 1-2 weeks
- **Monthly Operating Cost:** $0-20 (free tier hosting)
- **Projected Monthly Revenue (Year 1):** $5,000-20,000
- **Break-even:** Month 1

## 🔧 Technology & Operations

### Tech Stack:
- **Backend:** FastAPI (Python) - Fast, modern, async
- **Database:** PostgreSQL - Reliable, scalable
- **Cache:** Redis - Performance optimization
- **Payments:** Stripe - Industry standard
- **Hosting:** Railway/Render/DigitalOcean - Cost-effective
- **Monitoring:** Sentry + Logging - Reliability

### Data Sources:
1. **Trinidad & Tobago:** Companies Registry (public)

3. **Barbados:** Corporate Affairs Registry

## ⚠️ Risks & Mitigation

### Technical Risks:
- **Data accuracy:** Manual verification + user feedback
- **API downtime:** Multiple hosting providers + monitoring
- **Scaling issues:** Microservices architecture ready

### Business Risks:
- **Competition:** First mover advantage + network effects
- **Regulatory changes:** Multiple data sources + legal counsel
- **Payment failures:** Multiple payment methods + dunning emails

### Market Risks:
- **Slow adoption:** Free tier + developer marketing
- **Economic downturn:** Essential service for compliance
- **Currency fluctuations:** USD pricing + local payment options
